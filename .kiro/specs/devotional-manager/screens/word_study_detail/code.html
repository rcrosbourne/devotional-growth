ἀγάπη

Agape — The Highest Form of Love

Transliteration agápē
Core Definition

In the New Testament, agape refers to a sacrificial, unconditional love—the kind of love God has for humanity. It is not a feeling that happens to us, but a deliberate choice of the will to seek the highest good of another regardless of their merit or response.
